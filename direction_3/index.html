Design for All

In the philosophy of "The Egg" , the universe is revealed to be a single soul living every human life. Every stranger is simply you in a different time, facing different circumstances. If we view design through this lens, the barrier between "us" and "them" dissolves.

Design for All is not just charity; it is an act of radical empathy towards ourselves.

When we design for the aging (Article 1), we are simply preparing the world for our future selves. When we democratize design thinking (Article 2), we are acknowledging that creativity is a shared consciousness, not an exclusive privilege. And when we break the boundaries of our professional titles (Article 3), we refuse to limit the infinite potential of who we can become.

We are the user, the maker, and the medium. To design for one is to design for the whole.
